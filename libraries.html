Libraries & Tools

Build high- and low-level Interledger applications.

High Level: Wallet Client

Build Interledger payments into your apps with the Wallet Client. This client library connects to the five-bells-wallet, which uses the Simple Payment Setup Protocol for account discovery and automatic payment detail negotiation.

(COMING SOON) Low Level: ILP Client

Build Interledger payments into other protocols or low-level applications with the ILP Client. This library gives you direct control over the Interledger parameters and allows you to determine how to handle account discovery, condition negotiation, and communication between senders and recipients.
