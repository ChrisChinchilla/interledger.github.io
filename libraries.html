Libraries & Tools

Build high- and low-level Interledger applications.

High Level: ILP Client (SPSP)

Build Interledger payments into your apps with the ILP SPSP Client. This library can connect to the ILP Kit, which uses the Simple Payment Setup Protocol for account discovery and automatic payment detail negotiation.

Low Level: ILP Client (PSK, ILQP)

Build Interledger payments into other protocols or low-level applications with the ILP Client's PSK and ILQP functions. This library gives you direct control over the Interledger parameters and allows you to determine how to handle account discovery, condition negotiation, and communication between senders and recipients.
